I'm

a multidisciplinary designer, programmer, and maker in sunny San Francisco.

I founded and sold a software startup called Riskopy a few years ago. Since then my work has been focused primarily on the intersection of visual design and UI development, with an emphasis on strategy, user experience, and front-end prototyping.

Previously, I ran a small studio that specialized in designing and developing a strategic web presences for independent businesses. I was also the in-house designer for a social enterprise coffee company in Southeast Asia.

Today, I'm bringing my work back to my roots. I’m committed to applying my experience – in designing software, growing companies, and creating with persistent curiosity – to doing work that’s sustainable and community-oriented.
